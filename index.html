Frequently Asked Questions

What makes ClimatBloc different from traditional construction methods?
ClimatBloc's patented self-tensioning interlocking frame system and eco-friendly materials offer superior insulation, fire resistance, and wind resistance while significantly reducing the environmental impact of construction.
How does ClimatBloc contribute to sustainability?
Our hemp lime composite and carbon black infused concrete reduce CO2 emissions, while our efficient construction process minimizes waste. The high insulation value of our buildings also leads to long-term energy savings.
